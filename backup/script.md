Unlike CNNs, which look at small, local patches of an image, Vision Transformers use a mechanism called **'Self-Attention.'** This allows the model to understand the **global context** of an image. It doesn't just check if the eyes look real; it checks if the light on the eyes matches the light on the background across the entire frame. Research in early 2026 shows that ViTs now significantly outperform CNNs in detecting 'unseen' deepfakes.

Finally, we use **Hybrid Bi-LSTM models** for narrative analysis. These don’t look at the image; they look at the **language**.
