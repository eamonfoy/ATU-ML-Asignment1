### **Slide 1: The New Frontline – Cognitive Warfare (2026)**
**Goal:** Establish the "Information War" as a technical challenge, not just a social one.

* **Visual Elements:**
    * **The "Hybrid Warfare" Venn Diagram:** Create a diagram with three overlapping circles: **Psychological** (Emotions), **Informational** (Data), and **Technological** (AI/ML). Label the center intersection as **"Cognitive Warfare."**

* **Key Text/Data:**
    * Define **"Epistemic Chaos"**: The goal of making truth irrelevant.

### **Slide 2: The Technical Engine – Detection vs. Generation**
**Goal:** This is your "Mark Earner." It shows you understand the actual Machine Learning.

* **Visual Elements:**
    * **Vision Transformer (ViT) vs. CNN Diagram:** Show a side-by-side comparison. On the left, a CNN looking at small "pixel patches." On the right, a **Vision Transformer** with arrows showing "Global Attention"—how it looks at the relationship between the eyes, hair, and background all at once.
    * **The "Heartbeat" Visual:** An image of a face with a thermal-style overlay or a pulse-wave graph next to it. Label it **"rPPG (Remote Photoplethysmography)"**—the technology that detects blood flow to spot fakes.
* **The "Mark-Winning" Formula:**
    * Include the **Self-Attention Formula** in a small box at the bottom: 
        $$Attention(Q, K, V) = softmax\left(\frac{QK^T}{\sqrt{d_k}}\right)V$$
    * *Tip:* You don't need to explain the whole formula, just mention it as the "mathematical core that allows the AI to see the 'big picture' of a deepfake."

**Goal:** Show critical thinking by discussing the limitations of ML.

### **Slide Design Pro-Tips:**
* **Dark Mode:** Information War topics look best on a dark background (Dark Blue or Black) with neon blue or white text. It feels "cyber-technical."
* **Less is More:** Use bullet points, not paragraphs. Your **7-minute script** provides the detail; the slides provide the "proof."
* **Consistency:** Use the same font (like **Inter** or **Roboto**) on all three slides to look professional.
